Float Switch Specifications
Water that fills your dishwasher lifts the float. When it reaches a certain level, the float triggers the float switch to shut off, thereby shutting off the flow of water into the dishwasher.
This part fixes the following symptoms: Will not fill with water | Leaking | Not draining
This part works with the following products: Dishwasher.
This part works with the following products: Frigidaire, Kenmore, Crosley, Gibson.
Part# 154773201 replaces these: AP4982393, 1864244, 154209801, 154386601, 154408701
