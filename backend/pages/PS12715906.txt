BASKET,DOOR Specifications
This is a right-side door basket designed for use within LG refrigerators. This door basket is used to hold whatever condiments, containers, and food items are necessary. This basket is made of clear plastic and measures approximately 13.5 inches wide, 8 inches deep, and 3 inches tall. If your current refrigerator door basket is broken, cracked, or simply support any food items, it must be replaced immediately. To replace this shelf, simply lift the old one off its rear hooks, and install the new one by pushing its hooks into the door. Depending on your model, you may require more than one of this shelf, so consider that before ordering. This door basket is sold individually and does not come with any other parts.
