Door Shelf Bin Specifications
This is a door shelf bin made for use within some models of refrigerators. This shelf bin is used to hold food items and containers, but if it is cracked or damaged and no longer able to hold any items, it needs to be replaced immediately. This shelf measures 15-inches wide at its widest point, and 13-inches wide where it would be installed, as well as 6.5-inches deep and 3.5-inches tall. This shelf bin is an OEM part, and does not come with any other shelves or parts.
