Door Switch Assembly Specifications
This door switch is for dishwashers. The door switch detects when the door is closed so the dishwasher can start. Unplug the dishwasher or shut off the house circuit breaker before installing this part. Wear work gloves to protect your hands.
This part fixes the following symptoms: Door latch failure
This part works with the following products: Dishwasher.
