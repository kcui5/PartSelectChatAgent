Tine Holder Specifications
This plastic holder is also known as a tine clip, or a rack flip tine clip. It is used in the lower rack of a dishwasher, it is part of the rack assembly that allows to convert your rack to better the dishes you need washed. When you select the position you want the tines to be in, this clip hold the tines in place. This clip is designed to hold two tine rows in place. This tine clip is simple to install, you do not need any tools, it simply snaps into place on the dishrack.
