Recess Trim Black Specifications
This part is a replacement recess trim for your refrigerator. It is the trim that runs around the perimeter of the ice/water dispenser. You will want to replace the recess trim if it is cracked or damaged. This part is black in color and is sold individually. It is approximately 16 inches long and 10 inches wide. It is a genuine OEM replacement part sourced directly from the manufacturer.
