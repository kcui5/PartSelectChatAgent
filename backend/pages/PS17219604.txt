RACK ASSEMBLY UPPER Specifications
The upper dishrack is used to hold your dishes in place while the dishwasher goes through the wash cycles. Over time the dishrack can become damaged, start to rust, or be missing rollers, and the dishrack will need to be replaced. This rack is white in color with light gray rollers and measures approximately 23 Inch x 23 Inch. This is a genuine OEM part.
