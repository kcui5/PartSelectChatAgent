Lower Rack Tine Clip Kit Specifications
This tine clip kit is designed for use in the lower rack of dishwashers. Some dishwashers have adjustable tines that allow you to convert the rack in your dishwasher to accommodate more dishes, and dishes that are different shapes and sizes. Once you have the tine rows in the desired position, these clips help hold the tine rows securely in place for the washing cycle. This lower rack tine clip kit includes four clamps, two latches, and two L-shaped sleeves. The clips are made out of gray plastic. Installation is easy, no tools are required, these parts just snap into place on the dishrack.
