Dishwasher Drain Pump Specifications
This drain pump is for dishwashers. Drain pump removes the water from the dishwasher tub during the drain phase of the dishwashing cycle. Unplug the dishwasher and shut off the water supply before installing this part. Wear work gloves to protect your hands.
This part fixes the following symptoms: Not draining | Leaking
